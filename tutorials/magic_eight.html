Magic 8

1. Introduction

In this tutorial we will make a simple Magic 8 ball that you can use on your phone!

By the end you should be able to ask the Magic 8 ball a yes or no question and get a response when you click on it in your app.

Before starting, make sure you connect your app to your companion device. To do so go to the Connect tab in the top display bar and click on AI companion.

2. When the ball is clicked...

Drag the when magic_8_ball.Click block onto your viewer.

With this block we will tell the magic 8 ball what to do when we touch/click it in our app

3. Set the text

When the Magic 8 ball is clicked we want it to display a new message to us.

We will update the message by setting the text of the Magic 8 ball when it is clicked. Do this by dragging out the set magic_8_ball.text to block.

4. List of responses

The Magic 8 Ball will have a list of responses to choose from to display when you ask a question.

Start by dragging out the make a list block.

Now we will populate the list with responses. Drag in text blocks and write the words "Yes" and "No" as the responses.

Bonus: Can you add more responses? Hint: Click the blue gear on the make a list block.

5. Random response

The Magic 8 Ball should give the player a random response. To do this, let's use the pick random item block.

The last step is to combine the blocks we have!

Put the blocks together so that the Magic 8 Ball gives a random response when the user clicks the ball.

Test your code! Does the Magic 8 Ball work on your companion?
