Mole Mash

1. Introduction

In Mole Mash your mole is constantly moving. It moves when you tap on it and it moves if you don't tap it quick enough! You get points when you tap the mole. For this game we will be:

  • Keeping count of the score
  • Moving the mole to a random location
  • Using the timer to tell the mole when to move
