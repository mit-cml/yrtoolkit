Hello Bonjour Translator

The Challenge

Youth Radio ran this story about a women's immigrant mother and how she struggles to connect with other parents because she's not confident with her English.

You're challenged with creating an app that could act as an aid for her and other immigrant parents who need a little extra help in English-speaking situations. We can't wait to see what you come up with!

Click connect your app to start building your app!

1. Introduction

You've started with a screen with a few components already added.

2. Go to Blocks page

Click on the Blocks button on the top right of the screen.

3. Add click handler

Click on translateButton and add when translateButton.Click.

4. Request translation

Click on translateButton and add call YandexTranslate1.RequestTranslation to your when translateButton.Click block.

5. Add empty string

Click on Text and add the " " block to the "languageToTranslateTo" socket of your call YandexTranslate1.RequestTranslation block.

6. Add language codes

Click on your " " block and enter the text, "ru-en".

This is how the codes work.

Click here to see the list of all supported language codes.

7. Get text to be translated

Click on the russianText label and connect russianText.Text to the "textToTranslate" socket of the call YandexTranslate1.RequestTranslation block.

8. Get the translated text

Click on YandexTranslate1 again and drag out the when YandexTranslate1.GotTranslation block.

9. Reset the label

Click on the englishText label and drag out the set englishText.Text to block and connect it to when YandexTranslate1.GotTranslation.

10. Get the translated text

Hover over the red translation button and grab and click on get translation. Connect this block to set englishText.Text to.

11. Try and expand your app!

Try out your app on your companion device! When you press the "Translate" button, does the translated text appear?

Next, go to the "Expand your app" section below and add more features to your app!
