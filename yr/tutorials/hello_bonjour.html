The Challenge

This is Youth Radio's story from Amanda Agustin, who--like so many first-generation kids--has served as her mom's translator since she was little, which isn't always easy. “Yes, I’m bilingual,” she says, “and I can stamp that on my resume. And yes, it’s also a struggle to be the voice in the family at such a young age.”

You're challenged with creating an app that could act as an aid for families who need a little extra help in English-speaking situations. We can't wait to see what you come up with!
