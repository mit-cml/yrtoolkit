1. Connect with your device over wifi

Follow these steps to connect using the Companion app so you can test your app on your mobile device.

If you already know how to connect, connect the Companion app and click on the tab below to start the tutorial.

If you don't have a device to test with, click here and if you don't have wifi available, click here.

2. Download and install the MIT AI2 Companion App on your device

If you don't already have the MIT AI2 Companion app on your device, download it from the Play Store by scanning the QR code below or by opening this link on your device.

If you can't use the Play Store go to this URL http://appinv.us/companionon your device to download the Companion app.

3. Go through the installation instructions on your phone or tablet

After downloading, step though the the instructions to install the Companion app on your device. You need to install the MIT AI2 Companion only once, and then leave it on your phone or tablet for whenever you use App Inventor.

4. Open your app's QR code

Click Connect, then AI Companion.

5. Connect your app with your phone

A QR code similar to this should pop up on your screen.

Either scan the QR code or enter the code in the text box.
